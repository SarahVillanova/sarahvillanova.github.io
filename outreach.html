Science Outreach

I’m passionate about sharing physics and astronomy with the general public. Since 2021, I have been a member of Coffeeshop Astrophysics, a science outreach initiative run by graduate students and postdocs from the Center of Gravitation, Cosmology and Astrophysics at the University of Wisconsin–Milwaukee.

If you’d like to learn more about Coffeeshop Astrophysics, visit our website or check out the APS article about our work. Below you’ll find a few of the talks I’ve been part of.

Ancient Astronomy — Sep. 23, 2023 (co-presenter: Sashwat Sardesai)

The Science of Science Fiction — May 6, 2023 (co-presenter: Simon Yeung)
